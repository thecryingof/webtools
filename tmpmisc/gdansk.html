This part of the city is sometimes referred to as the Royal Route, since it was once the former path of processions for visiting Kings of Poland. Walking from end to end, sites encountered on or near the Royal Route include: - Highland Gate (Brama Wyżynna) - Torture House (Katownia) - Prison Tower (Wieża więzienna) - Mansion of the Society of Saint George (Dwór Bractwa św.
